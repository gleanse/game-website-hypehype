Castle Escape

One Castle One Goal Escape

Game Overview

Castle Escape is a fast-paced runner-action game where players take control of a ninja escaping a heavily guarded castle.

After being caught trying to steal a sacred scroll, the ninja must run through various castle zones, defeating guards using a sword and throwing shuriken, while avoiding direct contact with enemies.

The game introduces a refreshing blend of classic runner mechanics and interactive combat. Levels have a finish line instead of being endless, making each stage a mission to survive and escape.

Game Story

On a moonlit night, a rogue ninja slips into the castle vault and steals a sacred scroll. But his luck fails ‚Äî a patrolling knight spots him and sounds the alarm. The halls erupt with shouts as guards give chase. The ninja darts past piles of treasure and tables, into the castle's torch-lit corridors. Spike traps, swinging blades, and armored knights block his every step. He knows there is only one way out ‚Äî the main gates.

Through the grand halls and stairways he runs, until at last the night sky opens above the castle courtyard. Knights swarm faster, collapsing floors and traps threaten to end his escape, yet the forest lies just beyond the walls. But freedom is not so close. At the gates stand the castle's greatest defenders ‚Äî towering giants, sworn to crush any who dare approach the exit. Their massive blades block the only path out.

The rogue ninja grips his weapon. To escape, he must fight with every ounce of skill, defeat the guardians, and throw open the massive doors. If he succeeds, the gates will burst open, revealing the forest beyond. He will vanish into the shadows, the scroll in hand, leaving only whispers of the night the rogue ninja escaped the castle.

Characters

NINJA

A master of stealth and speed, the rogue ninja is a lone infiltrator who dares to steal the sacred scroll from the castle vault. Agile and cunning, he relies on precision and instinct to evade traps and guards. His only goal is to escape into the forest before the gates close forever.

KNIGHTS

Elite armored warriors sworn to protect the castle. They patrol the halls with vigilance and respond swiftly to threats. Though slower than the ninja, their strength and numbers make them dangerous foes in tight corridors and stairways.
